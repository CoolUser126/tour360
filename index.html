Why Visit Nepal?

Majestic Himalayas

Home to 8 of the world's 14 highest mountains including Mount Everest, the tallest peak on Earth.

Rich Culture

Discover centuries-old temples, monasteries, and UNESCO World Heritage Sites of the Kathmandu Valley.

Diverse Wildlife

Explore national parks housing rare species like Bengal tigers, one-horned rhinos and red pandas.

Seasonal Collections

Discover Nepal through our curated seasonal experiences

Summer Escapes

Cool retreats in Pokhara, Bandipur and Nagarkot with lush landscapes and mountain vistas.

Autumn Adventures

Perfect trekking weather for Everest, Annapurna and Langtang regions.

Winter Wonders

Cultural tours in Kathmandu Valley and wildlife safaris in Chitwan.

Popular Destinations

Kathmandu

The capital city known for its historic Durbar Square, Swayambhunath Stupa (Monkey Temple), and bustling Thamel district.

Cultural Heritage

Pokhara

The gateway to the Annapurna Circuit with beautiful Phewa Lake and stunning mountain views.

Adventure & Nature

Everest Region

Visit the base camp of the world's highest mountain or take scenic flights over the Himalayas.

Trekking

Lumbini

Birthplace of Lord Buddha, featuring sacred gardens, monasteries from different countries, and the Maya Devi Temple.

Pilgrimage

Chitwan National Park

UNESCO site famous for jungle safaris to spot rhinos, elephants, tigers and over 500 bird species.

Wildlife

Bhaktapur

Medieval city with well-preserved architecture, pottery squares, and famous Juju Dhau (King Curd).

Heritage

Cultural Highlights

Traditional Dance & Music

Experience diverse Nepali culture through traditional dances like the Mask Dance and Newari performances accompanied by unique instruments like madal and sarangi.

Vibrant Festivals

Participate in colorful festivals like Dashain, Tihar, Buddha Jayanti, and Indra Jatra which showcase Nepal's religious harmony and cultural richness.
