Cultural Dances of Nepal

Experience the Richness of Nepalese Dance Traditions

Nepal's cultural dances are a vibrant expression of its rich heritage, showcasing the diversity of its communities. Each dance tells a story, reflects traditions, and brings people together in celebration. Here are some of the most notable cultural dances from various ethnic groups in Nepal.

Lakhey Dance (लाखे नाच)

A wild, demon-like dance performed during Indra Jatra, Lakhey is considered a protector of children and the community.

Dance Highlights:

  • Origin: Kathmandu Valley
  • Features: Red mask, long hair, loud drums
  • Significance: Protector of children and community

Dhime Dance

A rhythmic dance performed in groups during festivals like Gai Jatra and Biska Jatra, featuring the Dhime drum and cymbals.

Dance Highlights:

  • Origin: Kathmandu Valley
  • Instruments: Dhime (double-headed drum), cymbals
  • Type: Group performance with rhythmic stepping

Maruni Dance

A joyful dance performed during Tihar and weddings, where men dress up as women and dance with enthusiasm.

Dance Highlights:

  • Region: Eastern and Western Nepal
  • Instruments: Madal, Sarangi, and singing
  • Occasion: Tihar, weddings, and celebrations

Sorathi Dance

A circular dance that tells the story of a queen and her twelve sisters, performed with singing folk ballads.

Dance Highlights:

  • Region: Western Nepal (Lamjung, Gorkha)
  • Performance: Circular dance with traditional costumes
  • Costume: Traditional Gurung dress and ornaments

Chyabrung Dance

A high-energy dance with martial moves, performed during the Udhauli and Ubhauli festivals.

Dance Highlights:

  • Region: Eastern Nepal
  • Instruments: Chyabrung (a special drum)
  • Style: Energetic dance with martial elements

Deuda Dance

A circle dance performed by men and women, symbolizing unity and friendship during Dashain and Tihar.

Dance Highlights:

  • Region: Karnali & Far Western Region
  • Style: Call-and-response singing and dancing
  • Occasion: Dashain, Tihar, and local fairs
