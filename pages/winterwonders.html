Chandragiri Hills

Chandragiri Hills, located on the outskirts of Kathmandu, offers a serene escape into nature. The area is known for its stunning panoramic views of the Himalayas, lush greenery, and rich biodiversity. Visitors can enjoy hiking, picnicking, and exploring the local flora and fauna, making it a perfect destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike.

places Highlights:

  • - cable car ride with snow at the hill top.
  • - The Bhaleshwor Mahadev Temple
  • - The panormic view of surrounding hills
  • - Majestic Himalayan mountain range

shivapuri

Shivapuri, a serene national park near Kathmandu, is a haven for nature enthusiasts and trekkers. The park is home to diverse wildlife, lush forests, and scenic trails that lead to the Shivapuri Peak. Visitors can explore the tranquil surroundings, enjoy birdwatching, and experience the rich biodiversity of this protected area.

places Highlights:

  • - Peaceful forest trail with Buddhist stupas and birdwatching
  • - Scenic views from Shivapuri Peak
  • - Diverse flora and fauna
  • - Tranquil picnic spots
  • - Rich cultural heritage

Chisapani Ridge

Chisapani Ridge, located just a few hours' trek from Kathmandu, offers breathtaking views of the Himalayas and a chance to experience the tranquility of nature. The ridge is a popular spot for both local and international trekkers, providing a perfect getaway from the hustle and bustle of city life.

places Highlights:

  • Forested trail through Shivapuri National Park
  • Offers views of snow-dusted Langtang and Himalayan villages
  • Stunning view of sunset
  • Peaceful atmosphere with local tea houses
  • Rich biodiversity and birdwatching opportunities

Khopra Danda Trek

Khopra Danda Trek, a hidden gem in the Annapurna region, offers stunning views of the Annapurna and Dhaulagiri mountain ranges. This less-traveled trail takes you through charming villages, lush rhododendron forests, and pristine landscapes, making it a perfect choice for adventurous trekkers seeking tranquility and natural beauty.

places Highlights:

  • Stunning view of Annapurna and Dhaulagiri mountain ranges
  • Charming villages along the trail
  • Lush rhododendron forests
  • Pristine landscapes and natural beauty
  • Rich biodiversity and birdwatching opportunities
